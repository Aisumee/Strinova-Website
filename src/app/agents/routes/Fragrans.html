Fragrans

Role: Support

Background

A floral expert and master perfumer, she is gentle, charming, and meticulous in life.

Skills

Active Skill: Aroma Surge

Type: Enchance

Fragrans emits one of two perfumes, creating an aromatic field around her. Allies inside gain an increased rate of fire or movement speed. The effect fades over time.

Details

  • Charges: 3
  • Duration: 12
  • Max rate of fire bonus: 12.5%
  • Max movement speed bonus: 12.5%
  • Radius: 20
  • Effectiveness is halved for Fragrans
  • Ignores line of sight

How to play

Passive Skill: Elixir Aura

Type: Healing

Fragrans releases a healing aroma to slowly restore HP to herself and nearby allies.

Details

  • Healing: 0 + 2/sec
  • Radius: 20
  • Ignores line of sight

How to play

Ultimate Skill: Euphoric Aroma

Type: Enchance

Fragrans creates a potent aromatic field. Allies inside gain increased rate of fire, movement speed, and HP regeneration. The effect doesn't fade. While active, Fragrans can't use weapons but receives significant damage reduction.

Details

  • Cost: 6 Ultimate Points
  • Healing: 0 + 15/sec
  • Damage Reducation: 50%
  • Radius:20
  • Ignores line of sight
