Michele

Role: Senitinel

Background

Michele is a rookie P.U.S investigator in the world of strinova. She is a gamer that applies her gaming to her job as a investigtor. Michele is guided by her seniors Kokona and Nobunaga. Michele loves cat and uses cat robots to fight the enemies, these are also known as Pawtector and Mighty Meow. She wishes to become a admired hero like her father who under unknown circumstances has fallen for collpase syndrome.

Skills

Active Skill: Pawtector

Type: damage

Michele depolys a Pawtector that can stick to any surfaces from a certain range. The Pawtector will automatically attack the enemy once they get into the range of the pawtector. The Pawtector will deactive once michele reaches a certain range away from it.

Details

  • Amount: 2/3 with upgrades
  • Cooldown: 40
  • Cooldown starts when a pawtector is destroyed or when all is placed
  • Hp: 45
  • Attack Range: 8
  • Summon Range: 25 meters

How to play

Passive Skill: Kitty Tracer

Type: detect

Allows her to see enemies that hits her with any weapon

Details

  • The detectation only lasts for 2 minutes and can only be seen by Michele.

How To Play

Ultime Skill: Mighty MeowBlast

Type: damage

Michele throws a powerful turret into the battlefield that auto-attacks nearby enemies

Details

  • It can only attack one enemy at a time
  • Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
  • Mighty Meowbot attacks in 2 phases. The first phase last around 1.25 seconds and deals 2 damage 15 times. The second phase involves a 0.1 charge time before doing 45 damage to the enemy
  • Mighty Meowbot can die in 2 ways. The first way is the moment that it is thrown, there is a 32 second countdown before it despawns. The second way is if it is destroy by enemy, Meowbot has 750hp.
  • Mighty Meowbot attack range is its line of sight, which allows it to attack from very far distances with a maximun range of 50 meters.
