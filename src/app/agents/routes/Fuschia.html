Fuschia

Role: Duelist

Background

A detached mercenary hired by Urbino. Beneath her calm exterior lies a fierce warrior. When deployed on missions, she wears a special tactical mask that absorbs Bablo energy to enhance her physical abilities

Skills

Active Skill: Apex Predator

Type: Detect

Description: Fuchsia exposes nearby enemies, visible only to her. Full HP enemies appear briefly, while those under 100 HP remain visible for the skill's duration. Exposing enemies boosts her movement speed.

Details

  • Cooldown: 25 Seconds
  • Duration: 7 Seconds
  • Increased Movement Speed: 15
  • Reveal duration on enemies with 100 or more: 1.35

How to play

Passive Skill: Hunter's Feast

Type: Healing

Enemies that are killed or assisted by Fuschia drops an energy crystal, which can absorb once she gets near it. Once abosrbed she will restore hp, and get increased max hp the first time she abosrbs an energy crystal

Details

  • Absorb radius: 14
  • Bonus health: 30
  • Does not apply healing while Combat state
  • Base total healing: 75(+15/sec)
  • Total healing with upgrade: 100(+25/sec)
  • Healing rate: 5/sec
  • Does not reduce healing limit when health is already full

How to play

Ultimate Skill: Bloodbath

Type: Enchance

Fuchsia greatly increases her rate of fire, tech power, and enables her active skill to illuminate all enemies within the field of vision. Energy Crystals absorption extends this boost.

Details

  • Cost: 6 Ultimate Points
  • increased firerate: 25
  • Increased weapon usability: 25
  • Bonus duration: 10(up to 5 times)
