Purgatory

Type: Enchance

Eika summons a spherical fire cage that burns traversing enemies, which will also extend the cage's lifespan. Eika's primary weapon deals additional fire damage to enemies within while Eika is also in the cage. 100 flame: increases cage damage.

Purgatory

Details

  • The fire cage needs 2.5 seconds to form and deals damage only after being formed. Lasts around 5.5 seconds.
  • The fire cage has radius 10 meters.
  • Enemies that enter or leave the fire cage will receive 30 damage (60 when buffed by full heat).
  • When both Eika and the enemy is in the cage, each shot's damage is increased by 2.

Sol Battery

Type: Enchance

Each shot on an enemy accumulates Heat up to 100. At max Heat, DMG against stringified enemies increases. Other skills can consume Heat for boosts.

Sol Battery

Details

  • Both the weapon being used and the part hit will affect the amount of heat gain. Ninjato does not accumulate any heat.
