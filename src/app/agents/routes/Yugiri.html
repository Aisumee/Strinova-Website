Yugiri

Role: Controller

Background

A girl haunted by a tragic past and a grim destiny. In her most recent escape attempt from the laboratory, she caused a corrosive gas leak, which ultimately resulted in an explosion. P.U.S currently has her in custody under the guise of supervision

Skills

Active Skill: Corrosive Mist

Type: Damage

Yugiri deploys a snail-shaped device that, upon activation, releases a spherical mist that temporarily erodes the armor of nearby enemies, reducing their defenses for a short time. This effect makes them more vulnerable to attacks. Up to three of these devices can be placed on the field at once, allowing for great control.p>

Details

  • Charges: 2
  • Cooldown: 45 seconds
  • Activation delay: 1 second
  • Armor corrosion: 25 + 10 per second (5 ticks per second)
  • Does not affect temporary armor

How to play

Passive Skill: Toxin Burst

Type: Damage

After dealing enough HP damage to an enemy with her primary weapon, corrosive toxins burst from the target, temporarily eroding the armor of nearby enemies.

Details

  • Damage requirement: 50
  • Armor corrosion: 25
  • Damage requirement with upgrade: 1

How to play

Ultime Skill: Venomous Formation

Type: Damage

Yugiri releases a sweeping mist that moves steadily moves forward, gradually eroding the armor cap of any enemies caught in its path.

Details

  • Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
  • Cast time: 2
  • Duration: 20
  • Armor corrosion: 25+10/sec(5 ticks/sec)
