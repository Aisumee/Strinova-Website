Phantom Mirage

Type: Weaken

Flavia summons an ephemeral butterfly follower, which gains temporary invincibility and generates a Phantom Orb upon taking damage. The Phantom Orb blocks enemy vision and can be destroyed.

Phantom Mirage

Details

  • Phantom Orb HP: 100
  • Cast time: 0.5
  • Phantom Orb duration: 3
  • Buff duration: 5
  • Cooldown: 25
  • Transformation can be ended early by pressing Active Skill button
  • Transformation can not be cancelled before Phantom Orb fully appeared

Phantom Mirage

Retribution

Type: Enhance

Damage taken reduces Flavia's active skill's cooldown. Taking a lot of damage grants her temporary ultimate points, which must be spent in the current round.

Retribution

Details

  • Self damage doesn't trigger passive effect
  • requires 200 damage to the character per temporary ult point, damage to orbs don't count

Phantom Flutter

Type: Rebirth

Flavia generates a Phantom Zone at her location, allowing her to transform into a butterfly for a brief duration after receiving an otherwise lethal blow from an enemy within the zone. Attacks from outside the zone will not trigger this effect. Flavia can only reveal herself to enemies outside the zone by attacking.

Phantom Flutter

Details

  • Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
  • Cast time: 1
  • Duration: 10
  • Transformation healing: 100
  • Leaving Phantom Zone area ends skill effect early
  • Transformation effect remain after ultimate ends

Phantom Flutter

Primary Gun: Solo

A light-weight submachine gun with an unusually high rate of fire and precision compared to most other weapons in its class. Equipped with a 1.25x scope.
