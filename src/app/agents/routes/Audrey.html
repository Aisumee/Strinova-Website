Audrey

Role: Sentient

Background

After the betryal of her uncle, the reputation of the grove family diminishes. Audrey,The current head of the Grove family wishes to bring glory back to the grove family. wielding supreme power in Urbino, she believes that firepower equals justice.

Skills

Active Skill: Guns Blazing

Type: Shield

Enables deployment mode to greatly increase rate of fire and avoid recoil and reloading. Audrey's excessive fire will overheat the muzzle, and she will be unable to shoot.

How to play

Passive Skill: Royal Shield

Type: Deploy

Audrey summons a frontal shield upon entering deployment or ADS mode with extra strength in the latter.
