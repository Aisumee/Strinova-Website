Nobunaga

Role: Senitinel

Background

Leading the First investigation Squad of P.U.S is Captain Nobunaga. Haunted by the betreyal of his own brother, Reichii, Nobunaga's pursuit of truth is relentless and exhaustive.

Skills

Active Skill: Guardian Eye

Type: enchanced

Nobunaga can deploy up to 2 Guardian Eyes. While near a deployed Guardian Eye, he benefits from increased fire rate, better weapon usability, and armor regeneration. The bonuses are amplified when two Guardian Eyes are connected.

Details

  • The guardian eye has a cooldown of 20 seconds and a AoE radius of 10.
  • The effects of the guardian eye only works on Nobunaga
  • Effect is stackable but the ability enchancement can't be stacked
  • The cooldown will start once a Guardian Eye is recycled

How to play

Passive Skill: Blinding Shot

Type: Weaken

When Nobunaga can charge up her shots when shooting in ads. These fully charged shots cause enemies to have increase shoulder fire spread and lose their crosshairs with reduced accuracy in ads mode.

How to play

Ultimate Skill:Pulse Overload

Type: Detect

Nobunaga deploys a pulse generator that emits waves in a 360 degree radius, revealing struck enemies to all teammates and applying silence for a duration.

Details

  • This ultimate last 16 seconds with a 2 second cast time
  • Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
  • With 100 hp, the pulse generator can be destroyed, ending its effect early
