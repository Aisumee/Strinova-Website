Ming

Role: Duelist

Background

The vice-captain of The Scissors' strike team, a valiant warrior, now one of The Scissors' spiritual leaders. She's equipped with an electromagnetic arm capable of manipulating electric currents to perform a variety of attacks

Skills

Active Skill:

Type: Damage

Ming fires an anti-armor lightning orb that explodes upon reaching its maximum distance, collision with an enemy Superstring, or when the skill is recast, leaving behind a magnetic field that slows all enemy units (including summons) and damages their armor. Ming gains movement speed within the field.

Details

  • Cooldown: 25
  • Duration: 3
  • Cast Range: 8 - 60
  • Orb Radius: 5
  • Damage: 20 + 20/sec

How to play

Passive Skill:

Type: Armor

Damage from Ming's firearm or active skill against enemy armor or shields automatically regenerates Ming's armor. This passive will also regenerate temporary armor faster during Ming's ultimate.

Details

  • Armor Damage Amplification: 10%.
  • Lifesteal: 20%(30% with upgrade)
  • Ultimate Lifesteal: 40%
  • Effectiveness decreased to 10% when damaging summons(does not affect ultimate).

How to play

Ultimate Skill:

Type: Enchanced

Ming's shots apply a stackable slowing debuff while she gains additional temporary armor. Damage from Ming's firearm or active skill against enemy armor extends the duration of the skill and regenerates temporary armor.

Details

  • Cost: 6 Ultimate Points
  • Bonus Armor: 100
