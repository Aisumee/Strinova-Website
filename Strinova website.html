About

Strinova is a game created by a chinese company called IDreamSky. It was globally released on November 21 2025. It is a tactical shooter game with many unique agents and Mechanics

Lore

After a great catastrophe befell old Earth, humanity relocated to a multidimensional realm called Strinova. Here, beings known as Superstrings can effortlessly switch between 2D and 3D forms, and battle endlessly for the future they envision.

Agents

P.U.S

The official government of the new world, maintaining order while striving to find a cure for "Collapse Syndrome

Scissors

An armed group opposing P.U.S is attempting to seize control of Bablo Crystals to open a portal back to the old world.

Urbino

A commercial syndicate controlling the industries of the new world, exploring ways to reach other dimensions using crystal energy.
