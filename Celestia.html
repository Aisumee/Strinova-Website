Celestia

Role: Support

Background

Oringinally wokring for the P.U.S, she leaves after the appearence of the Collapse Syndrome. she founded Astral Sanctuary to heal those affected by the disease, and is being funded by urbino to help with her research

Skills

Active Skill: Guardian Star

Type: Armor

Celestia targets an allied character with a String Meteor, granting them temporary armor. The Meteor will then return to Celestia and grant her temporary armor.

Details

  • Effect can stack
  • Cooldown: 25
  • Bonus temporary armor: 30(target) + 20(self). Capped at 100 shield
  • Loses armor at a rate of ⛊3/sec

How to play

Passive Skill: Cosmic Recovery

Type: Healing

Celestia gradually heals her armor and nearby allied characters armor

Details

  • Aura radius: 15
  • Ignores line of sight
  • Healing: ⛊2/sec

How to play

Ultimate Skill: Astral Gate

Type: Teleport/Buff

Celestia boosts her and an ally's armor while charging an instant teleport to their location.

Details

  • Teleportation can be canceled by tapping ultimate while charging (without affecting the armor boost)
  • Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
  • Temporary armor: 100(target) + 100(self)
