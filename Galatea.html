Galatea

Role: Vanguard

Background

A renowned magician and a mad artist hiding behind a beautiful appearance. She is also the older sister of Maddelena

Skills

Active Skill: Wild Card

Type: Teleport

Galatea throws a card in an arc. Upon impact with the ground or a wall, it creates a decoy. Press the interact key to teleport to the decoy's location.

Details

  • Cooldown: 20 without upgrades
  • Duration: 7 without upgrades

How to play

Passive Skill: Deception

Type: Decoy

Galatea leaves behind a decoy whenever she unsticks from a wall. This passive only goes on cooldown if a decoy takes damage. Her decoys briefly detect nearby enemies not hidden behind obstacles, revealing them to her team. Enemies attacking the decoys are also temporarily exposed.

Details

  • Duration: 7 seconds without upgrades
  • Can be destroyed by abilities and Guns
  • Cooldown when wall-decoy is damaged: 10 without upgrades
  • Making a new wall-decoy, the previous one will disappear; more can exist by using her ultimate and active skill
  • Making a wall-decoy is loud

How to play

Ultimate Skill: Mirage

Type: Teleport

Galatea throws serval cards, each decoy allowing for teleportation

Details

  • Cost: 5 Ultimate Points
  • Duration: 15 Seconds
  • 3 cards are thrown, 75 degress apart
