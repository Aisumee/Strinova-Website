Yvette

Role: Controller

Background

Yvette is a brilliant P.U.S researcher who always carries her stuffed teddy bear, “Fay,” wherever she goes. Despite her introverted nature, she holds a strong trust in her friends. In battle, she uses Fay to lock down the battlefield, suppressing the enemy’s movements.

Quiet, shy, and reserved, Yvette is a researcher at P.U.S. Though she may seem distant on the outside, she has a warm heart and cares deeply for those close to her. With the aid of Fay, her trusty bear, she summons freezing frost that immobilizes and traps her enemies.

Skills

Active Skill: Teddy Assault

Type: Summon

Yvette summons Fay, a controllable teddy bear that creates an icy surface upon colliding or on command, causing slipping and amplifies damage taken.

Details

  • Radius: 10m
  • Deploy time: 2 seconds

How to play

Passive Skill: Camouflage

Type: Stealth

Yvette becomes Camouflage after standing still for a period of time. She remains invisible from distant enemies and gradually becomes visuable the closer the enemies are.

How to play

Ultime Skill: Frostfall

Type: Weaken

Yvette summons a Frost Bear that creates 3 Polar Zones in succession, causing enemies to slip, slowing their fire rate, and reducing weapon usability.

Details

  • Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
  • Debuffs: Fragile, Slippery, Rate of Fire Reduction, Weapon Usability Weakened
