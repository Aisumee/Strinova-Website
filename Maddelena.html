Maddelena

Role: Controller

Background

A scatterbrained artist who joined urbino with great interest with the world around her. Given the lack of combat experience, Urbino developed a special spray gun allowing maddelena to play a suuportive role on the battlefield

Skills

Active Skill: Pigment Bind

Type: Weaken

Maddelena covers the target zone in paint, slowing, string-blocking, and exposing the tracks of traversing enemies.

Details

  • 2 charges without upgrades
  • Cooldown: 20
  • Duration: 15 Seconds
  • Can be splashed on walls and can splash through walls
  • Can collide with enemies projectiles resulting in the paint being splashed on the spot where they collided
  • Paints of opposing teams take effect simultaneously

How to play

Passive Skill: Paint Tracks

Type: Detect

Hits from maddelena primary weapons allows her to expose enemies tracks and slow them down

Details

  • Duration: 2

How to play

Ultimate Skill: Paint Bubble

Type: Weaken

Shoots a paint bubble that string-blocks and exposing tracks of enemies within. Once exploded, it deals additional slowing and damage amplifcation effects against enemies within

Details

  • Cost: 7 Ultimate Points
  • Duration: 15 Seconds
  • Damage bonus: 35%
  • Pop debuff durations: 5 Seconds
